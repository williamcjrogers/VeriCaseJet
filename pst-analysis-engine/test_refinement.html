How the Refinement Wizard Works:

    
    
        
  1. Discovery Phase: Analyzes uploaded PST emails to find:
            
      
                
    • Other project references (to exclude irrelevant emails)
      • 
                
    • Organizations and their roles (Employer's Agent, Council, etc.)
      • 
                
    • Key people and their email domains
      • 
                
    • Important topics (delays, payments, defects, etc.)
      • 
            
    
        
    2. 
        
  2. Interactive Q&A: The wizard asks smart questions like:
            
      
                
    • "I noticed references to 3 other projects. Should I exclude these?"
      • 
                
    • "Based on the emails, Calfordseaden appears to be the Employer's Agent. Is this correct?"
      • 
                
    • "Haringey Council sent 456 emails. Are they the local authority for this project?"
      • 
            
    
        
    3. 
        
  3. Apply Filters: Once confirmed, the wizard:
            
      
                
    • Marks excluded emails in the database
      • 
                
    • Updates the project with confirmed party information
      • 
                
    • Saves the refinement configuration for future reference
